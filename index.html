About the Exam

What is MRB Exam?

The MRB Exam is a state-level test by the Tamil Nadu Medical Services Recruitment Board (MRB) to hire medical professionals. It fills roles in the Health and Family Welfare Department of Tamil Nadu.

Eligibility Criteria

Candidates must have a BDS degree from a recognized university, completed a one-year internship (CRRI), and be registered with the Tamil Nadu Dental Council. Proficiency in Tamil is required, and government employees need a No Objection Certificate (NOC).

Learn More

Application Process

Applications are submitted online through the Tamil Nadu MRB website, with an accurate entry of details and online fee payment based on category. A valid email and mobile number are required, and once submitted, applications cannot be modified.

Learn More

Exam Pattern & Dates

The exam has a 50-mark Tamil Language Test (qualifying) and a 100-mark BDS Subject Test (CBT mode in English). There is no negative marking, the exam lasts three hours, and dates are updated on the official MRB website. Exam dates to be announced.
